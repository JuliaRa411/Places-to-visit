    Pomona Mills Park

    Nature park featuring forests, meadows & wetlands with walking trails, a stream & wildlife.

    Pomona Mills Park Loop is a 2.1 kilometer moderately trafficked out and back trail located near Markham, Ontario, Canada that features a river and is good for all skill levels.

    The trail is primarily used for walking and running.

    Today, Thornhill is a vibrant, mature residential area with a portion reflecting the heritage of the area mentioned above.
